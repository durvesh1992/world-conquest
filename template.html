World Conquest

A real world map, 39 regions, 25 great powers. Train forces, launch invasions that cross the map in real time, and watch the borders shift — until only your color remains.

Build — select your territory and queue Infantry 🪖, Fleets 🚢, Air Wings ✈️. Training takes time; more land means more factories.
Attack — click your territory, then a highlighted neighbor. Your force physically travels there, then the battle rages until one side breaks.
Defend — incoming invasions show up in the Threats panel with who's attacking and where. Reinforce, or hit them back at home.
Advance — age up your empire and buy unit upgrades to outclass older armies. Train in bulk with ×5 / ×10 / Max, or hotkeys 1 / 2 / 3.
Win — every playable nation has a unique specialty. Paint all 39 regions your color to rule the world. Progress auto-saves.
