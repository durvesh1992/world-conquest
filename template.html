World Conquest

A real world map, 44 regions, 25 great powers. Train forces, launch invasions, drop nukes, choke sea lanes — and watch the borders shift until only your color remains.

Build — select your territory and queue Infantry 🪖, Fleets 🚢, Air Wings ✈️. Training takes time; more land means more factories.
Attack — click your territory, then a highlighted neighbor. Your force physically travels there, then the battle rages until one side breaks.
Defend — incoming invasions show up in the Threats panel with who's attacking and where. Reinforce, or hit them back at home.
Advance — age up to unlock nukes ☢, raise monuments 🏛, blockade coasts ⚓, and demand tribute 💰. Superpowers span several regions — take them piece by piece.
Win — capture a nation's capital ★ to steal 40% of its specialty. Paint all 44 regions your color to rule the world. Progress auto-saves.
